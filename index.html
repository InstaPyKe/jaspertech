Core Expertise

Deep technical proficiency across the full security and development stack.

Web Security

Web Security is the cornerstone of protecting digital assets. My expertise lies in rigorous penetration testing and identifying vulnerabilities within complex applications. By adhering to the OWASP Top 10 framework, I ensure that critical risks like SQL injection and cross-site scripting are systematically mitigated. I employ manual and automated techniques to simulate real-world attacks, providing comprehensive assessments that go beyond surface scanning.

Networking

My expertise in Networking centers on deep infrastructure analysis and protocol inspection. I specialize in designing and auditing network architectures to ensure they are robust against lateral movement. By analyzing traffic patterns and deep-packet data, I identify anomalies indicating potential breaches. Whether configuring firewalls or implementing zero-trust policies, I ensure the underlying transport layer remains impenetrable and highly available.

Secure Programming

I specialize in Secure Programming, leveraging Java, Go, and Python to create applications that are secure by design. My approach involves implementing robust input validation and encrypted data handling at every layer of the development lifecycle. I prioritize writing clean code that minimizes the attack surface. By staying updated on language-specific vulnerabilities, I ensure software can withstand modern exploitation techniques in enterprise environments.

CEH Certified

As a Certified Ethical Hacker, I approach security from a hacker’s perspective to stay ahead of malicious actors. This encompasses offensive tactics like reconnaissance and gaining access to identify weaknesses. Beyond offensive strategies, I am skilled in digital forensics, allowing me to investigate breaches and identify root causes. This dual capability helps clients defend systems while preparing for effective incident response.
